# Multimodal Prehabilitation in Cancer Care: Development of Evidence-Based Nutritional Pathways and Educational Resources

## Project Overview

This repository documents a professional placement project undertaken as part of the MSc Health Data Science programme at University of Aberdeen in collaboration with Dr Monika Gostic Nutrition Ltd.

The placement focuses on translating research evidence into practical nutritional support pathways and educational resources for individuals before undergoing cancer treatment.

## Project Aim

To develop evidence-based nutritional support pathways and educational resources for individuals before cancer treatment through the systematic identification, appraisal, synthesis, and communication of evidence from prehabilitation interventions.

## Placement Activities

* Literature searching
* Evidence extraction
* Critical appraisal
* Evidence synthesis
* Evidence mapping
* Data visualisation
* Development of patient resources
* Development of clinician resources
* Development of educational resource library 
* Scientific writing and dissemination

## Planned Outputs

* Systematic Review
* Evidence Table
* Evidence Map
* Patient Educational Resource
* Clinician Educational Resource
