# PICO Table

| Component            | Inclusion Criteria                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              | Exclusion Criteria                                                                                                                                                                       |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Population (P)**   | Adult patients (≥18 years) diagnosed with cancer (colorectal, esophagogastric, gastric, lung, pancreatic, liver, urologic, gynaecologic, breast, or mixed cancers) undergoing surgical resection.                                                               | Studies involving paediatric populations, non-cancer patients or patients not undergoing surgical resection were excluded.                                                               |
| **Intervention (I)** | Multimodal prehabilitation implemented in the preoperative period, comprising at least two interventions, including exercise (aerobic and/or resistance training) and nutritional support (dietary counselling and/or protein/oral nutritional supplementation) | Unimodal prehabilitation intervention or multimodal prehabilitation without either nutrition or exercise component or interventions implemented exclusively in the postoperative period. |
| **Comparator (C)**   | Standard perioperative care, postoperative rehabilitation, enhanced recovery after surgery (ERAS) protocols alone, or usual care without a structured prehabilitation component.                                                                                | Studies without a true comparator group                                                                                                                                                  |
| **Outcomes (O)**     | At least one of the following: functional capacity (e.g., six-minute walk test, cardiopulmonary exercise testing), postoperative complications, length of hospital stay, quality of life (HRQoL) or nutritional status/body composition                         | Studies not reporting any of the predefined outcomes                                                                                                                                     |
| **Study Design**     | Randomised Controlled Trials (RCTs); published between 2016 and 2026; published in English; human studies; full-text articles available                                                                                                                         | All other study types; studies published before 2016; publications in languages other than English; animal studies; studies without full-text article available                          |
