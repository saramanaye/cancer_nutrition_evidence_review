# Search Strategy

## Review Title

Optimal Delivery of Multimodal Prehabilitation for Adults Undergoing Major Cancer Surgery: A Systematic Review

## Review Aim

To identify and synthesise evidence relating to the delivery and effectiveness of multimodal prehabilitation interventions for adults undergoing major cancer surgery.

## Databases Searched and Search Strategy:

**Date searched:** 16 June 2026

### PubMed

```text
(prehabilitation[tiab] OR pre-habilitation[tiab] OR "preoperative rehabilitation"[tiab])
AND
(cancer*[tiab] OR neoplasm*[tiab] OR tumor*[tiab] OR tumour*[tiab] OR malignan*[tiab] OR oncolog*[tiab] OR carcinoma*[tiab] OR "Neoplasms"[Mesh])
AND
(surger*[tiab] OR operat*[tiab] OR resection*[tiab] OR perioperative[tiab] OR surgical[tiab])
```
